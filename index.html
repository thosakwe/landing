About Me

Hello! I'm Tobe.

I'm a 19-yr-old polyglot programmer who's been coding since the age of 7. I believe in free, equitable access to information, and giving back to the community.

In the past few years, in addition to freelance gigs, I have published over 300 open-source projects on Github.

My hobbies include recording and producing music, exercising, and writing.

My eventual goal, after building a successful career, is provide others with the same opportunities I have had, by donating time and funds to the education of underserved communities.

Music

Game of Love EP (2017)

The first project I ever released on SoundCloud, Game of Love is a collection of 3 songs about love, with a bonus politically-themed song.

The production is admittedly amateur, but in terms of composition, it offers a diverse sound with several high points.

Revolution of Evolution (2018)

Riding on two samples from Sister Sledge's "Thinking of You," "Revolution of Evolution" is a song about ending negativity and judgment towards others, and embracing the fact that we are imperfect and ever-changing.

It shifts from disco/R&B to a funky rap, and then finally ends with about a minute of ad-libbing and scatting.
